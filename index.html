Features

Penguins App has five functional modules: short video account, mall, live broadcast, chat square, and application center.

Short Video

Through big data, artificial intelligence technology automatically matches accurate short video content for users based on their clicks, browsing, hobbies, and updates popular and exciting videos in real time to ensure distribution efficiency. Users are also able to shoot short videos or upload clips after secondary creation.

Mall

Relying on platform traffic and technical advantages, it provides local entity merchants with an integrated online and offline platform. Compared with physical malls, Penguins Internet Mall is highly intuitive and interactive, which can significantly reduce product sales costs and increase product exposure rate for merchants.

Live Broadcast

Live broadcasting has entered the multi-dimensional development of entertainment, diversification, stardom, and commercialization nowadays. With the unique e-commerce and blockchain combination of the Penguins Short Video App, its powerful communication capabilities, instant, interactive and transparent features for the "host +" model will further release the value of the webcast industry.

Chat Square

Penguins short video app will be the first traffic open interactive system, where users can chat freely and transparently with other users, learn about advisory, and set up communities. Each user may find their own community, bloggers and other team leaders to quickly establish their own fan community.

Penguins short video as a new ecological short video new benchmark industry, it is bound to break the traditional closed data system and adhere to the technical concept of openness, transparency, and non-tamperability, making the "Penguins Short Video App" to become a rewarding and worthwhile short video social platform that everyone wants and be able to participate in.
